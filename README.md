# Meshworks
Meshworks is a gear-driven factory puzzle about routing torque, powering belts, and growing your workshop.


Meshworks is a gear-driven factory puzzle about building motion, power, and profit from a single rotating source. You begin on a hex-based grid with a rotor and a generator, then place small and large gears to create working gear trains that carry torque across the workshop. Every placement matters: gear size affects transmission, inefficient links reduce output, and careless layouts can lock the entire drivetrain. The result is a puzzle game where mechanical logic and spatial planning are at the heart of every decision.

Once the system starts producing power, the game expands into a compact factory simulation. Electricity feeds conveyor belts, outbound machines, packers, power strips, and trucks, all of which must be connected and managed carefully. Packages move down the line, get packed, loaded, and shipped for profit, turning your successful mechanical designs into a growing production network.

As your funds increase, you can upgrade nearly every part of the workshop. Boost rotor torque, improve small and large gear efficiency, raise generator efficiency, speed up belts, increase outbound throughput, shorten packing time, expand truck capacity, and improve truck speed. You can also add more production lines, stacking extra conveyor systems above or below the original and sharing upgraded power strips across multiple lines to support a denser, more efficient layout.

The goal is to reach 100000 funds, but clearing that milestone is only the beginning. After the stage is completed, you can keep playing, optimizing your design, expanding throughput, and refining the balance between mechanical transmission and factory logistics. Meshworks blends tactile gear-placement puzzles with satisfying automation growth, creating a factory-building experience centered on motion, planning, and elegant systems.
